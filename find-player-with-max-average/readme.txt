By the given data of players with their respective score, find the max average held by a player.

input data

[
    {name: 'tom', score: 1},
    {name: 'sam', score: 1},
    {name: 'tom', score: 2},
    {name: 'sam', score: 3},
    {name: 'karthik', score: 5},
    {name: 'tom', score: 4},
    {name: 'sam', score: 3}
];


output: karthik. (He who player only 1 game and has a average of 5)

Tom: 1 + 2 + 4 / 3
Sam: 1 + 3 + 3 / 3
Karthik: 5 / 1
